# Hi, I'm Julian San Diego

I'm an EECS student at UC Berkeley focused on power electronics. I'm passionate about building the hardware behind renewable energy and electrification—from high-frequency converter topologies and PCB design to embedded controls.

Currently, I work as an undergraduate researcher at the **Berkeley Power and Energy Center (Boles Lab)**, where I investigate advanced control strategies for high-power-density piezoelectric based DC-DC converters. I thrive at the intersection of first-principles hardware design and practical lab bring-up.

When I'm not in the lab winding custom magnetics or analyzing Bode plots, you can usually find me bouldering or folding [origami](origami.md).
